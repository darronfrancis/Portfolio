ABOUT

I believe that life is measured by the number of lives that you positively impact. I've lived with that commitment while directing music & technologies in churches for over 20 years, helping people find their homes as a real estate agent, and creating/designing websites, videos, promo materials, and training courses for companies.

I've lived in several states including FL, MD, MS, PA, MO, and IN, as well as globally including India and Malaysia. I've also visited over 20 countries. This globetrotting experience has given me a great appreciation for different cultures and individuals from all walks of life.
